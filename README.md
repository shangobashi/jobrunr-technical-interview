## JobRunr Open Job position

We are currently hiring for a full-time position as a Junior Software Engineer. 

If you are interested in joining our team, you can apply __only__ if:
- you have a valid working permit for Belgium
- you're living in Belgium and are willing to come to our offices in Leuven (next to the railway station) for 4 days per week initially and 2 days per week after your first review (about 3 months)
- you can solve the unit test in this repo within 15 minutes without the use of internet. This means without using
  - any external libraries or frameworks nor
  - any IDE LLM autocomplete features nor 
  - using ChatGPT or any other LLM coding model


If you match all above requirements, please send your resume and cover letter to [ronald@jobrunr.io](mailto:ronald@jobrunr.io).

The interview process will be as follows:
- a technical coding challenge for 30 minutes with a similar difficulty as the exercise here in our offices (without the possibility to use internet)
- a debriefing session discussing your solution with me (Ronald Dehuysser), the CTO of JobRunr
- a meet and greet with the team
